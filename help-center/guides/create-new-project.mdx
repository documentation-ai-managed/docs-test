## Overview

Easily create a new project in test to organize your docs.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to dashboard">
    Log in at `https://dashboard.test.com`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start new project">
    Click the "New Project" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Enter your project name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click "Create" to finish. Your project appears in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
